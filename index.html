MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI

Our Very Own Captain Cool

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, affectionately known as MS Dhoni, stands as a cricketing legend and former captain of the Indian national team. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Dhoni's leadership prowess and calm demeanor on the field earned him the moniker "Captain Cool." Under his guidance, India secured historic victories, notably winning the ICC World T20 in 2007 and the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011. Dhoni, known for his distinctive finishing skills and the iconic helicopter shot, left an indelible mark on the sport before retiring from international cricket in 2020.
